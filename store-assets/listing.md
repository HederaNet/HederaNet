## Full Description

HederaNet turns community members into infrastructure entrepreneurs. Deploy mesh Wi-Fi hotspots, trade solar energy peer-to-peer, and rent compute power — all secured on Hedera Hashgraph with instant on-chain payments.

**For Operators:**
- Deploy internet hotspots and earn HBAR from every subscriber
- List solar energy and sell kWh to neighbors (IoT-verified delivery)
- Stake HNET tokens for Bronze/Silver/Gold tier multipliers
- Track earnings in real-time with full on-chain transparency

**For Subscribers:**
- Find affordable internet plans in your area
- Buy verified solar energy directly from local producers
- Vote on network governance proposals
- Send/receive HBAR and HederaNet tokens

**Key Features:**
✓ Hedera Hashgraph — 10,000+ TPS, carbon-negative blockchain
✓ HashPack & Blade wallet support
✓ Works on 2G USSD (*384*1#) — no smartphone required
✓ Multi-language: English, Yoruba, Hausa, Swahili
✓ All transactions publicly verifiable on HashScan
