# Outline

HederaNet is a Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Network (DePIN) built on Hedera Hashgraph that enables African communities to own and monetize their own internet and energy infrastructure. Community members deploy WiFi hotspots and solar energy installations, earn HBAR for providing those services, and govern the entire platform together through on-chain proposals and voting.

Whether you are looking to earn income by operating infrastructure, access affordable connectivity and clean energy as a subscriber, or shape the network's future through governance — this documentation is your complete guide.

***

## Who Is HederaNet For?

| Role                        | What You Do                                                    |
| --------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Operators**               | Deploy hotspots, list solar energy, stake HBAR, earn rewards   |
| **Subscribers**             | Purchase internet access and solar energy from local operators |
| **Governance Participants** | Hold HNET, create proposals, vote on network decisions         |

***

## Why Hedera Hashgraph?

Hedera Hashgraph gives HederaNet properties that no traditional blockchain can match:

* **Speed** — Transactions finalize in 3-5 seconds, so payments and service activations are instant.
* **Low cost** — Each transaction costs roughly $0.0001, making micropayments for energy and internet economically viable.
* **Environmental sustainability** — Hedera is carbon-negative, making it a natural fit for a platform built around renewable energy.
* **Enterprise governance** — The Hedera Governing Council (Google, IBM, Boeing, and others) provides stability and regulatory clarity.
